GameJam

My Game Jam game "The snail foundation"
is a game whereyour goal is to get as many
snails to your house as posible. There are
all sorts of upgrades to help you with
Reaching your goal, like multispawn,
More snails per snail, and a rebirth system
where all your progress is reset for a boost.

This Project was created in my seccond
year of software development during The
Dutch Design Week. it was a small project
we worked on for around 3 days. tyhe goal
was to make something in a short amount of
time We had to choose betweeen different Themes
and me and my groupmate chose snails in gardens
